Survey Questions

📘 I like to read books.

📖 I read books at home.

🧸 I like to read with my teacher.

🧍‍♀️ I like to read by myself.

👂 I like to listen to my teacher read aloud.

🧠 I learn from books I read.

📚 I choose books that are interesting to me.

🙋 I ask questions about what I’m reading.

🎉 I am proud when I read well.

💬 I talk about what I read with others.

👨‍👩‍👧 My family reads books.

🤝 My friends like reading.

👀 I see reading posters and displays in my classroom.

📅 We have regular time to read in class.

📈 Reading helps me learn new things.

📚 I know how to choose a book that’s right for me.

🎨 I enjoy talking or drawing about books.

📕 I like when I understand what I read.

✍️ I enjoy writing about books I read.

🎭 I like acting out stories I read.
